Why I’m Building a New Job Board for New Zealand and Australia

Job hunting is already a tough and emotional process — especially in a competitive market like New Zealand or Australia. Yet in 2025, many of us are still using outdated platforms that haven’t kept up with how people actually search for jobs today.

I’ve personally felt this pain while job hunting: irrelevant listings, poor filtering, outdated UI, and slow results. And after talking to friends, colleagues, and recruiters — I realized I’m not the only one.

What’s wrong with the status quo?

  • Clunky interfaces and slow loading times
  • Filters that don’t work (or don’t exist)
  • No way to easily find remote roles or visa-sponsored positions
  • Outdated or duplicate listings

What I’m building instead

Findwise is a job board designed from the ground up to solve these issues. Here's how:

  • AI-powered search: Find relevant listings faster, with smarter matching.
  • Modern, minimal UI: Easy to navigate on mobile and desktop.
  • Powerful filters: Remote-only, part-time, contract, sponsorship — all at your fingertips.
  • Transparent employer tools: No blind ads. Real listings with real info.

Why now?

Tech is advancing quickly, and job seekers are more tech-savvy than ever — but job boards haven’t evolved. I believe it’s time for a better experience tailored to how we actually search for work in 2025.

Want to help shape it?

I’ve launched a simple early-access landing page to gather feedback and interest. If you're a job seeker or employer in NZ or AU, your voice will help shape this from the start.

Thanks for reading — and for supporting independent tools built for real people.
